Cafe

Good food, Good coffee, Good karma.

1. Hauz Khas Social – Wonderful Ambiance

They have earned their place as one of the best cafes in Hauz Khas owing to their world-class acts, super consistent quality, tempting cocktails, fabulous music, and the most wonderful ambiance that will make you wanna stay here forever. You must have caught a glimpse of this chic and elegant space in the Bollywood flick “Tamasha”, if you haven’t been there already. Apart from being one of the most rocking cafes in South Delhi, Social is also a dynamic working space that attracts office goers and freelancers looking for a quiet environment to work and conduct business meetings.

Where:9-A & 12, Hauz Khas Village, New Delhi

Zomato rating: 4.4

What’s special:Coffee, Lasagna, Salad, Mocktails

Price for two:1,300


2. Hard Rock Cafe, Saket – Sing-Along The Karaoke

As the name suggests, (and as every Dilliwala already knows) Hard Rock Cafe is where one heads when in the mood for some smashing, soul-satisfying music, limitless booze, and tasty burgers! Their playlist is always full of surprises and if you’re lucky, you might catch up to some famous bands like The Local Train playing there in the evening. The music, booze, and food options are the reasons this place is steadily climbing higher and higher in the list of the best cafes in Delhi.

Where:M-110, 1st Floor, DLF Place Mall, Saket, New Delhi

Zomato rating:4.2/5

What’s special: Burgers, Cocktails, Onion Rings, Mocktails

Price for two: 2,500

3. Delhi Heights, Chanakyapuri – Try The Best Of Delhir

One of the best late-night cafes in Delhi, Café Delhi Heights serves sinfully delicious waffles that are worth dying for. Their waffles come in abundance with a selection of whole wheat or plain dough in three flavors – banana and seasonal fruits with Chantilly toffee cream, cherries, and berries, chocolate crumble, cheese, and sweet potato, each bite of which is a blissfully sweet delight. Don’t forget to taste one of the best coffee in Delhi while you’re at Delhi Heights.

Where:9, Sardar Patel Marg, Diplomatic Enclave, Chanakyapuri, New Delhi

Zomato rating:3.9

What’s special: Burgers, Juicy Lucy Burger, Pasta, Pizza, Cocktails, Coffee, Mocktails

Price for two:2,000
