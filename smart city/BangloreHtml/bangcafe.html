Cafe

Good food, Good coffee, Good karma.

1. The Hole in the Wall Café, 8th Main Road, Kormangala

For those looking for good cafes in Bangalore, this is an ideal escape. Just like its funny name, The Hole in the Wall Café offers a diversified and thrilling menu of all day breakfast, waffles, continental and Italian cuisines. One of the best cafes to eat, read and relax, The Hole in the Wall, is a two-story café, that have bagged immense praises and fame from locals as well as travellers.

Where: Door No 4, 8th Main Rd, Koramangala 4th Block, Koramangala, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560047

Zomato rating: 4.6/5

What’s special:Mr. Hole’s Eggless Breakfast, Triple Chocolate Pancake, Mixed Sauce Pasta

Price for two: INR 600


2.Matteo Coffea, Church Street, Shanthala Nagar

If you’re looking for best coffee places in Bangalore, you must visit Matteo Coffea. It is hands down the most buzzing Bangalore cafes, known for authentic coffee and desserts. Famed as a speciality coffeehouse, this place uses the top-quality Arabica beans for the rich coffee taste. Though the food served here is super delicious and varied, this place offers the best coffee in town and has won the hearts of many coffee connoisseurs.

Where: Church Street, Indiranagar

Zomato rating: 4.6/5

What’s special: Hot Cocoa, White Sauce Creamy Pasta

Price for two: INR 600

3. Dr Sheesha, Deep Plaza, 1st Phase, J.P. Nagar

Dr Sheesha boasts of cozy rooftop ambience and super courteous staff. The café also brings you an array of vegetarian food from all over the world and tempting hookah menus. Besides food, helpful staff and pretty décor, the wide variety of sheesha flavours, served in attractive looking pots are the elements, that pulls crowd here. With an option of indoor and outdoor seating, this is among the best cafes in Bangalore for couples.

Where:Deepa Plaza, 17/17, 24th Main Rd, 1st Phase, J. P. Nagar, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560078

Zomato rating:4.2/5

What’s special:Wai Wai Chat, Blueberry Blend, Watermelon Crusher

Price for two:INR 800
