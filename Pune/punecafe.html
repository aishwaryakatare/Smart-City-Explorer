Good food, Good coffee, Good karma.

1. Hippie @ Heart

Living up to its dreamy name in every sense, Hippie@Heart is where you will rediscover the hippie residing in you. One look at this expansive restaurant in Pune, and you can't help but bowl over by its gorgeous interiors and decor. You can expect your eyes to feast on colourful murals of pop-culture icons painted across the walls, rustic wooden seating arrangements, and vibrant stained glass on every window nook. Topping off its undeniably charming ambience is food that tastes so good, you'll be licking your fingers long after you've left!

Where: SF 16, 2nd Floor, The Pavillion, SB Road, Senapati Bapat Road, Pune

Zomato rating: 4.6/5

What’s special: Daab Chingri, Guava Mocktail, Peri Chicken, Churros

Price for two: INR 1400

2. Mamagoto

The city's favourite hub of Asian food is as awesome for a foodie, as for budding photographers. You can spot gorgeous, Manga-themed decor everywhere you look, and all their colourful walls make for perfect backdrops for your Instagram photos. The delightful food at Mamagoto also features some excellent presentation, so that you can get plenty of drool-worthy snaps on top of your aesthetic shots of the interiors.

Where: Church Street, Indiranagar

Zomato rating: 4.6/5

What’s special: Cocktails, Mocktails, Spicy Shrimp, Crispy Shrimp

Price for two: INR 1500

3. The Sassy Spoon

An exquisite place to head to for some dinner and wine, The Sassy Spoon has its fair share of loyal fans in the city, and for good reasons. The moment you arrive, a bright pink vintage car will greet your eyes, which is only the first of the beautiful sights that await you. The interiors are replete with brick walls, shrubs of hydrangeas, beautiful low-hanging lights and vibrant pops of colour when you least expect them. The outdoor seats are great for kicking up your feet and relaxing with some mulled wine while your friends click "candid" shots of you!

Where: Lane 7, Sanskriti Lifestyle Complex, Koregaon Park, Pune

Zomato rating: 4.2/5

What’s special: Cocktails, Beer, Strawberry Smoothie, Cold Coffee

Price for two: INR 1600
